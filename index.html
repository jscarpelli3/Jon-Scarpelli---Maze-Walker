The Maze Tower

Help! Mazzy is stuck inside a tower of mazes and the only exit is at the TOP!!!

Guide Mazzy to each exit to reach the top of the tower.

BUT don't forget to find him a parachute so that he can jump when he reaches the top!

If you don't find it, Mazzy will fall to his death when he reaches the top (Oh No!!!).

Along the way, collect useful items and coins he can use to buy Beer when he gets out.

to MOVE:
Use the Arrow Keys.

to use TORCH:
Press 0 on the keypad

to use a LADDER:
press the arrow toward the wall you want to cross and then Press CONTROL

If you have a PLANK, you will automatically be safe from holes
